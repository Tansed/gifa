Rules for Gurgaon (Junior and Senior)

  1. The teams are divided into different pools. One pool contains 3 teams.
  2. Each team will get to play 2 matches.
  3. Team will be awarded 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw and 0 for a loss.
  4. We shall have pool toppers.
  5. The winning criterion for the pool topper will be maximum points scored, followed by highest goal difference, followed by maximum goals scored.
  6. The top 16 for pre-quarters will be selected from among the pool toppers basis the maximum points scored, followed by highest goal difference, followed by maximum goals scored.
  7. Post the selection of top 16, we do pre-quarters, quarters, semis and final.

Please Note –

  • In the event of any dispute, the decision of the HT GIFA management will be binding on all teams.
  • Handing a walkover and disqualification of teams is under the complete discretion of management.
  • On match days, we want the teams to be present at the venue 45 minutes before their match time.

Rules for Delhi (Junior and Senior)

  1. We will follow knockout format .
  2. Progress of a team depends on their continuous winning run.
  3. We do 3 rounds of knockouts to reach top 16 in Junior and Senior Category.
  4. The top 16 for pre-quarters will be selected after the 3 rounds of knockout basis the maximum points scored, followed by highest goal difference, followed by maximum goals scored.

Please Note –

  • In the event of any dispute, the decision of the HT GIFA management will be binding on all teams.
  • Handing a walkover and disqualification of teams is under the complete discretion of management.
  • On match days, we want the teams to be present at the venue 45 minutes before their match time.
